Our Story

Here is how we achieved our success through the words of our owner Clare.

I began my career as a Reflexologist having completed certificates in Holistic Medicine, Aromatherapy and the ancient art of reflexology. I have instilled into all my therapists the importance of treating clients in a typical holistic fashion taking into account Mind Body and Spirit. I began working on my clients in a small room attached to a Holistic Healing Centre. The work and surroundings were comfortable and rewarding. After a while I soon found out I needed to diversify into some different therapies. I trained at the Galligan Beauty College and my course included Facial Therapy and Body Massage.I studied the massage manipulations such as effleurage, stroking, petrissage, tapotement and vibrations. These techniques can be used in both facial and body message. The effects are wonderful they increase circulation, remove physical tension in muscles, stimulate the Lymphatic System to remove waste products more efficiently and break down fatty tissue. The whole effect improves texture of the skin and creates a feeling of well being. With this wealth of experience we guarantee you will leave after a session feeling refreshed and ready to meet all your tasks with renewed energy. Notes will be taken on medical conditions to rule out any reactions. Why not treat yourself, you deserve it.

Clare, Founder and Owner
